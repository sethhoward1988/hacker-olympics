Prizes

For our most recent puzzle, there are three top prizes and several Honorable Mentions. Your code will be judged by the following criteria. Top prize is $25.00 mailed directly to your door.

  • Top Prize: Whomever can complete the puzzle correctly in the fewest number of lines
  • Top Prize: Whomever can complete the puzzle correctly in the fewest number of characters
  • Top Prize: Whomever's code has the fastest clock speed.
  • Honorable Mentions: Those who complete the puzzle correctly with the fewest lines and characters for each language (Assuming they didn't win overall)
  • Honorable Mention: Anyone who completes the puzzle correctly using only SQL and a database.
